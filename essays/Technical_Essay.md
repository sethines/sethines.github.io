## Interests in software engineering

Being a computer science major, it is kind of assumed that you will constantly be learning. Software updates and patches can change almost daily which means that the learning in computer science never ends. I guess that is why I am drawn to it. I love the fact that it can, over the course of time, change to become something different. For example, the way that artificial intelligence is changing the world in every way. Some of the things in software engineering that I particularly love are the collection, analysis, and application of data sets or data engineers. Software engineering makes the process much easier than having to look at and determine the numbers and outcomes by hand. Figuring out how to make systems and processes faster is something that I really enjoy. Another reason why I really like data engineering is because it bridges the game between the raw data and the people that need to use it like scientists and the general consumers. It is something that can be used to help the public in general when it comes to decision-making or interpreting a situation. My second interest in software engineering is security engineering. It is fascinating how some people can brute force into computers, and others can prevent hacks from occurring. The main reason why these are my interest is that they seem to affect the average consumer the most in terms of daily life. It allows me to help the community more by being in these kinds of software engineering fields. 

## Development of skills

Currently, I am working as an IT Support Technician at Ka'elepulu Elementary School. Because of this, I have some experience in the front and back end of software engineering though not much. Some of the skills that I would like to develop would be getting more familiar with big data technologies, data warehousing, and cloud platforms. Having more knowledge in these areas would allow me to further my knowledge base for data engineering as well as get me one step closer to a job in that field. Other skills that I would like to develop are getting more familiar with security tools, threat modeling, and scripting. With these types of skills, my dream of being able to create, store, and protect my personal data on my own is possible. This has been a long-term goal of mine ever since I got into computer science. 

## Experiences

As for experiences, I hope that through the different experiences, I gain friendships that will carry on beyond college into our respective fields where people can just ask each other for help even though we may not be in the same companies. Other experiences that I would like to develop would be a part of both a red and blue team to see how each of them operates. This will help in bolstering the resilience of a network and the data that would be stored there. For the data science side, I would like to be a part of a team that develops a method to take raw data and translate it into data used for the general population. In general, I would like to develop more team problem-solving experiences since this can translate into almost any field.
